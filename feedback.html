Familiarity and Receptiveness to Acceptance and Commitment Therapy Therapy



Although 87% of respondents were unfamiliar with Acceptance and Commitment therapy, 66% were somewhat receptive to it and 27% were highly receptive to ACT therapy. This highlights the great potentional ACT therapy have on helping users stay motivated to eat healthier.

Current Usage of Food Logging vs Usage of Food Logging with



Due to forgetfulness and a lack of motivation and many other reasons, only 12.2% of respondents log their food data more than serveral times a week. Despite the low number of people actively logging their food data. The survey showed that with an AI bot to assist with this mindless and tedious task, 63.7% would be more likely to log food.
